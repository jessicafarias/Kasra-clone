Video made to fit

There is no one-size-fit-all video solution. Every business video must be made carefully to deliver your message perfectly.

Be it explainer video, motion graphics spot, corporate presentation or even a TV commercial, we never compromise on quality as we don’t just aim to do one project, but to start long term relationships.

Everything produced in our animation company is bespoke and carefully crafted from scratch by an award-winning team of designers & animators.

Pubquiz

Tickera

This 3D animation featuring cool characters with vibrant burst of colors was created for Tickera, an event ticketing system built specifically for WordPress websites. Tickera is an all-in-one solution, presented as a Swiss Army knife in the animation.

AirSwap

AirSwap team had very clear idea on what they wanted. A video style that suits the concept of blockchain and cryptocurrency to explain about their revolutionary token economy. “Everyone loves the video”, as quoted by Don Mosites, one of the founders of AirSwap.

Stanley

STANLEY Engineered Fastening is a STANLEY Black & Decker Inc. Company, with presence in a number of locations worldwide. For more than 40 years, Stanley Engineered Fastening has been revolutionizing fastening and assembly technologies. We helped them tell their story.

MegaGrass

In line with the revamp of its website, MegaGrass wanted to create a series of explainer videos to talk about how to measure lawns, why should people opt for artificial grass, the installation process and so on. We were thrilled to work closely with the MegaGrass team in developing fun 3D animations.
